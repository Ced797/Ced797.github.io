Heros and Villians of the Battlefront

Obi-Wan Kenobi

The legacy of the fabled Obi-Wan Kenobi transcends eras. He was a member of the Jedi Council, a general for the Galactic Republic, and later, after spending years in exile, mentor to Luke Skywalker.

Anakin Skywalker

Anakin Skywalker was believed to be the Chosen One, who would bring balance to the Force by ending the Sith.


Darth Tryannus

Serving the dark side, Count Dooku supervised the creation of the clone army on Kamino, trained General Grievous in the art of lightsaber combat, and educated the Dathomirian Asajj Ventress in his teachings.


General Grievous

A towering Kaleesh cyborg, the mere presence of General Grievous causes unease amongst his enemies.
